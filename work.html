My Work

Dinao

Currently, I am an active Backend Developer for the Dinao project.

I am responsible for the server-side functionality of the project, which includes parsing data, developing custom API integrations, managing the database, and much more. My role is to ensure seamless performance and scalability, providing users with a smooth and efficient experience.

Dinao is an innovative application designed to help users discover the best places to visit, including restaurants and entertainment spots, in Belgrade and Novi Sad. The app allows users to search for locations based on their preferences, explore menus with high-quality dish photos, and read authentic reviews from other visitors.

Currently, the app is available for download on the App Store, exclusively for iOS devices. Dinao aims to make finding the perfect spot for any occasion easier and more enjoyable than ever!
